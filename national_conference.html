2016 SASE National Conference & Career Fair

SASE National Conference in Dallas Oct. 13-15. Network and get internships and jobs. The Career Fair is Oct. 15 and is free. Rice SASE will subsidize but please fill out the interest forms below so we can get a head count.

PLEASE FILL OUT THESE TWO FORMS

  • Official Registration
  • Interest Form

Hello both new and returning SASE members!

Welcome to the new semester! Your officer team is currently in the midst of planning this semester's events. While we have yet to hold our first general meeting, we want to direct your attention to the following. Specifically, if you are pursuing next summer's internship or a job, we advise you to attend.

The SASE National Conference is the largest conference + career fair for Asian Americans in the US.

The entire conference (Oct. 13-15 in Dallas) includes:

  • - STEM Career Fair of 70+ companies, many of which are Fortune 500
  • - Professional and leadership development workshops, many of which are led by company representatives
  • - Networking events with industry professionals
  • - Meals + gala dinner!

Logistics (costs+ travel)

The entire conference costs $100 until 9/16, when the price will go up to $120. Along with travel and misc., we're expecting it to cost $200/ person. But not to worry- SASE Rice can help subsidize at least partially, if not all, costs. Here are the main ones you should consider:

Travel: We recommend carpooling or by bus-- plane tickets will not be 100% reimbursed. Keep your receipts (tickets, gas, etc) for subsidies.

Food: Meals included in conference price. Meals outside conference would be out-of-pocket and not sponsored.

Housing: $249 a night, but will be covered 100% by Rice SASE. We will be staying in Downtown Dallas, at the Hyatt Regency Dallas. (300 Reunion Boulevard). The hotel will be booked for Friday and Saturday night (10/14-10/15). 4 Rice people to a room, same gender unless otherwise noted.

However, note that there is the option to only attend the career fair, which is free and happens on Saturday, Oct 15.

Options: There are 2 links, one to the registration and one to the interest form.

  • 1. Whether you wish to attend the entire conference or only the Career Fair, fill out the registration and interest form.
  • 2. Those who register for the entire conference: keep the receipt so SASE can subsidize you for the registration fee. (Exact amount will be determined after interest form is filled out by everyone). Do NOT book a hotel by yourself. SASE will book hotels. Everyone who wants to go to Dallas must fill out the interest form so that our team can decide how to split up our funds to best help out everyone.

It would be best if you made your decision by Sept. 16, before prices go up.

Thanks and hope to hear from you soon! Many of us officers went last year and it was honestly worth all of it. Any questions, email Renee at rl35@rice.edu.

Best, Rice SASE
