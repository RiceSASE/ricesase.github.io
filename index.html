SASE Rice University

The Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers (SASE) is dedicated to helping students excel in various aspects, with student-governed chapters nationwide.

About

Rice SASE (Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers) is a chapter of the national organization by the same name. SASE is dedicated to helping STEM majors excel both academically and professionally, as well as positively impact the world around them. We have monthly general meetings, typically revolving around professional workshops and guest speakers from industry. SASE membership is open to men and women of all ethnic backgrounds!

Mission

SASE is dedicated to the advancement of scientists and engineers in education and employment so that they can achieve their full career potential. In addition to professional development, SASE also encourages members to contribute to the enhancement of the communities in which they live.
